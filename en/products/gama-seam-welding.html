Un-Gama Seam Welding Machines

Un-Gama Seam Welding Machines

Seam WeldingContinuousIntermittentAC/MF

Seam welding machines producing continuous or intermittent welds for liquid-tight and gas-tight joints. Preferred especially in tank, container and filter production. Manufactured with AC and MF technologies for precise and repeatable weld quality.

The Gama series provides a wide welding speed range of 0.5 to 5 m/min. Suitable for material thicknesses from 0.3 mm to 3.0 mm. Weld wheel diameter and width can be customized to suit each application, and the automatic cooling system ensures long-lasting production.
