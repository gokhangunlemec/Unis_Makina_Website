Type-3 Multi-Spot Welding Machines

Type-3 Multi-Spot Welding Machines

Multi-SpotSimultaneousCustom FixtureHigh Productivity

Multi-spot welding machines capable of performing 2 to 24 weld points simultaneously in a single cycle, designed for high-volume automotive production. Custom fixture solutions are prepared for each part. Significantly increases cycle time and productivity compared to single-spot welding.

Manufactured with AC and MF technologies. Serra welding control units ensure precise current distribution across all electrodes. Modular design facilitates fast changeover for different part types. Suitable for door panels, floor panels and body components.
