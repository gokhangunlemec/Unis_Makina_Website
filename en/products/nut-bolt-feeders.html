Nut & Bolt Feeders

Auto FeedVibrationFeeding System

Automatic nut and bolt feeding and driving systems used in automotive and industrial production lines. Provides fully automatic production by working integrated with welding machines and robot lines.

The system, consisting of vibration feeder bowl, guide rails and driving mechanism, reliably feeds nuts and bolts of different sizes and types to the welding position. Ensures production quality with misfeed detection and counter functions.
