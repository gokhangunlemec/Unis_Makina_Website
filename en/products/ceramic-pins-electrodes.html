Ceramic Pins & Electrodes

Ceramic Pins & Electrodes

ZrO2Si3N4Al2O3

Long-lasting and repeatable performance with high wear resistance, thermal stability and precise tolerance advantages. Specialized ceramic pins and electrodes used in projection and spot welding applications.

Ceramic pins manufactured from ZrO2, Si3N4 and Al2O3 materials provide electrical insulation during welding due to their non-conductive structure. They exhibit superior resistance to high temperatures and mechanical stress, offering a significantly longer service life compared to standard electrodes.
