Who We Are

27 Years of Experience
in Welding Technology

Unis Makina was founded in 1997 in Bursa and has been operating in the fields of resistance welding machines, welding control units and special machine manufacturing.

As the solution partner of Spanish Serra company in Turkey, we provide welding equipment, technical support and engineering services at international standards. Serra's 90+ years of experience combined with Unis Makina's local expertise creates a powerful synergy in the Turkish and regional market.

Partnership

Serra Soldadura
Turkey Solution Partner

We are the official solution partner of Serra Soldadura, a world leader in industrial welding technologies since 1930, in Turkey. We combine Serra's 90+ years of experience, advanced technology and wide product range with Unis Makina's local expertise and fast service network.

Through this partnership, we can offer our customers the latest welding technologies from Serra's Barcelona-based production facilities, along with local technical support and after-sales service.
